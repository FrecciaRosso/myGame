Match-Match Game

Welcome to Match-Match Game!

Your task is to find pairs for all cards. Click New Game to start. After that a timer will start running.

You can change the cards skin and game difficulty level in the settings at the top.

How to play:

  • Click on a card to turn it over and see the picture.
  • Pick another card to check if it matches the one you selected.
  • If the pictures are similar, you can proceed and find more pairs. Otherwise, it's computer's turn.
  • Player, who found more pairs, wins.

Good luck!
